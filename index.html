Babe ruth

Babe ruth who's real name is German Herman Ruth, jr was born on Feburary 6th, 1895 in pigtown, Baltimore. He married his wife Helen ruth from 1914 to 1929 and had a kid with her named Dorthy Ruth. His marriage would end in 1929 when his wife was killed in a house fire.

Babe Ruth played for 3 different teams which were the orioles in 1914, Boston red sox from 1914 to 1919, and the new york yankees from 1920 to 1934. In his early years he would attend an all boys school named St. Mary where he would learn different practical work skills like cooking, shoemaking and carpentry. Babe Ruth would join the baseball team and it had quickly became clear he had a natural talent for the game, During his high school years he would play multiple positions.
